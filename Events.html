The CEUS organizes a wide range of activities for Civil Students! We try our best to make all the events and activities inclusive and accessible.

Colloquium

Colloquium is the annual departmental trip hosted by the CEUS. The trip lasts 4 days and consists of industry tours as well as social events. The location varies every year but is usually a Canadian city within driving distance of Montreal. Past locations have included cities as close as Ottawa to as far as Fredericton. The trip happens at the end of September.

Civil Banquets

After a long semester of assignments, labs, and midterms, a banquet would be perfect as a break and celebration. In both fall and winter semester CEUS host a banquet for all civil engineering students. We celebrate the end of the semester with food, drinks, and laughters..

Civil and Sandwiches

Civil and sandwiches is an academic event that CEUS hosts at least once a semester. The CEUS VP Academic is responsible for planning and organizing the event. Professors from the civil engineering department are invited to speak about a topic relating to their research.

Students are invited to connect with their professors in an informal setting outside of the classroom, while at the same time eating sandwiches provided by the CEUS!

More info

Wing night

It is so popular that the Civil wing night become a traditional event that happens once per semester. Take a break from all the studying and come on out on Wednesday with fellow civvies and enjoy some delicious cheap wings.

Year socials

The UReps from each year organize social events, from taco events, to lasertag. This is the fun time to hang out with your classmate. You know we study hard, but also play hard. So just forget all the school work for a moment and enjoy some good time.
